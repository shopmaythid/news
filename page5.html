Political Activism

If we want to bring true and permanent change, we must unite and fix our society through legislation. Voting is a privilege and you must use that power to represent those who cannot vote yet. For those who are not old enough to vote, you can still become immersed in campaigns and become involved in our current political climate. 

Register to Vote

Register to Vote 

If you are over 18 years old and have still not registered to vote then do it today! If you are unsure if you have registered or not then you can check. The voter registration process only takes 2 minutes. 




Pre-Register to Vote

Pre-Register to Vote 

Still not 18 yet? You can pre register to vote and they’ll remind you to do it on your 18th birthday.






Fill out your 2020 Census today!

Fill out your 2020 Census today!

The 2020 Census helps ensure that resources are evenly distributed amongst the people and there is one census to fill out for every household. It is only available to fill out every decade and The Census Bureau includes every person living in the U.S. — regardless of citizenship or immigration status. Your voice matters!
